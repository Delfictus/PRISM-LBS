PARAMETER MEANINGS
================================================================================

THERMODYNAMIC (Phase 2):
  cooling_rate          How fast temperature decreases each step
                        0.92 = 8% reduction per step (smooth)
                        0.95 = 5% reduction per step (fast/aggressive)

  steps_per_temp        Iterations at each temperature level
                        100 steps allows more escape attempts

  guard_triggers        Count of energy barrier escape failures
                        <100 = healthy, >200 = unstable

  compaction_ratio      Degree of state space compression
                        0.9 = 90% compression, 0.0 = no compression

QUANTUM (Phase 3):
  coupling_strength     Strength of interaction term in Hamiltonian
                        8.0 = balanced, 12.0 = over-aggressive
                        Affects decoherence rate and artifact generation

  evolution_time        Duration of Hamiltonian evolution
                        0.15 = 150 milliseconds (sufficient for 125 nodes)
                        0.08 = 80 milliseconds (insufficient)
                        Scales approximately as sqrt(N) for N vertices

  purity                Measure of quantum state coherence
                        1.0 = pure state, 0.0 = completely mixed
                        Target > 0.93 indicates good quantum properties

  entanglement          Degree of quantum correlation between qubits
                        0.0 = no entanglement, 1.0 = maximum
                        0.88-1.0 = full resource exploitation

ENSEMBLE (Phase 7):
  diversity             Population diversity measure (0.0 to 1.0)
                        0.0 = all replicas identical
                        >0.3 = good exploration of solution space

  consensus_threshold   Fraction of replicas agreeing on solution
                        0.9 = strict agreement required
                        0.8 = allow some disagreement on details
